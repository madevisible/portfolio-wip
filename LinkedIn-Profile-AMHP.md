## ABOUT
*(2.600 tegn max — dette er ca. 1.400 tegn)*

I work at the intersection of language, structure and human experience — making complex systems navigable without stripping them of their nuance.

My background combines a Master's in Psychology of Language with hands-on experience in content design, information architecture and service analysis. I notice what users will struggle with before they encounter it: the flow that asks too much, the instruction that answers the wrong question, the interface that addresses itself rather than the person trying to move through it. And I notice what people already carry with them that has not been made visible yet — and build from there.

I am most drawn to work where language genuinely determines outcomes: public-sector digital services, accessibility-critical products, neurodiversity-informed design and the thoughtful integration of AI into human-centred workflows.

My AI workflow is deliberate and role-specific: Perplexity for research and source verification, ChatGPT for visual iteration and execution, Claude for structure, editing and document production. The ideas, the direction and the judgement are mine throughout.

I am based in Reykjavík and open to permanent or fixed-term employed positions, hybrid or remote.

Portfolio and work samples available on request.
